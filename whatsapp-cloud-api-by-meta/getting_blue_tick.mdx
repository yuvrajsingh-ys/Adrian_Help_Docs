## Overview

This guide walks you through connecting AIP with HelpShift in 5 steps — from logging in and finding your credentials, to mapping parameters and testing your first lead.

---

## Step 1: Access the HelpShift Integration in AIP

1. Log in to your AIP account.
2. Select the bot you want to integrate and click on the **"Integrate"** tab.
3. Go to the **"All Integrations"** tab.
4. Find **HelpShift** by scrolling the list or searching **"HelpShift"** in the search bar.
5. Click the **"Connect"** button.
6. You will be prompted to enter:
   - **Domain Name**
   - **API Key**

Both are obtained from your HelpShift account (see Step 2).

---

## Step 2: Get Your API Key and Domain from HelpShift

1. Log in to your HelpShift account.
2. From the left sidebar, click the settings icon and switch to the **"APIs"** tab.
3. Copy your **API Key** and **Domain** from this section.

---

## Step 3: Connect HelpShift to AIP

1. Enter the **API Key** and **Domain** in AIP.
2. Click **"Connect"**.
3. If entered correctly, you will see: **"Integrated Successfully."**

---

## Step 4: Map Parameters

1. Map the **HelpShift fields** to the corresponding questions in your AIP chatbot flow.
2. Use the **"+ Add Parameters"** button to add more field mappings.

**Example:** Map the question *"Please provide your name?"* to HelpShift's **Name** field. This ensures the user's response is sent to the correct field in HelpShift.

<Info>
Questions are auto-fetched from your bot's chat flow. Make sure your chat flow is configured before setting up mappings.
</Info>

3. Once all fields are mapped correctly, you will see: **"Mapping Successfully."**
4. The HelpShift integration will show as **"Installed"** in the integrations list.

---

## Step 5: Test Your Integration

Create a dummy lead on your bot. The lead should appear in both your AIP dashboard and your HelpShift account.

---

## Troubleshooting

If the dummy lead is not appearing in HelpShift, check the following:

- **Wrong Authorization Details** — Verify that the API Key and Domain are entered correctly.
- **Wrong Mapping Setup** — Ensure all mandatory HelpShift fields are mapped to the correct AIP questions.
- **Subscription** — Integrations are only available on paid plans. Verify your current subscription.

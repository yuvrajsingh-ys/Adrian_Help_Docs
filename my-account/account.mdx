## What is the Account Section?

The **Account** section in AIP allows you to manage and update your **personal details**, **contact preferences**, and **company information** — all from a single dashboard. Whether you're customizing your profile or updating your company's industry classification, the Account section keeps your data organized and ready for platform use.

---

## Benefits of the Account Section

- **Centralized Information** — Update all key account-related details in one place.
- **Business Profile Management** — Ensure your business contacts and branding are always current.
- **Company Insights Alignment** — Tailor your company information to match your industry and scale.

---

## How to Use the Account Section

1. **Log in to Your AIP Account** and navigate to the dashboard.

2. **Navigate to the Sidebar** — On the left-hand sidebar, scroll down and click on **"More"**, located just beneath the Help Center.

3. **Access My Account** — From the expanded menu, click on **"My Account"**. The **Account** tab will open by default.

4. **Edit Profile Information** — In the **Profile Information** card, you can update your **profile picture**, **name**, **email address**, **connected phone number**, and **preferred platform language**. Click **Submit** to save.

5. **Edit Primary Contact Information** — In the **Primary Contact Information** card, enter or update your **primary contact name**, **business title**, **business email address**, and **business phone number**. Click **Submit** to save.

6. **Configure Company Information** — In the **Company Information** card, you can update your **company name**, **industry** (e.g., Finance, Education, Food, Automotive), **company size** (e.g., 1–10, 10–100, 100–1000+), and **company website**. Click **Submit** to save.

## FAQs

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my email address here?">
    Yes, under **Profile Information**, you can update the email address associated with your login.

    It stores and manages your business's main point of contact for communication and preferences.

  <Accordion title="Can I change the language of the platform?">
    Yes, you can select your preferred platform language in the **Profile Information** section.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What industry options are available for company classification?">
    Options include **Finance & Banking**, **Food & Grocery**, **Education**, **Entertainment**, **Automotive**, and others.
  </Accordion>
